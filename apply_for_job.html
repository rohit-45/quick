Interested in becoming a Quickease Team Member?

At Quickease we aim to revolutionize the car wash industry. We work to provide the most convenient services possible because we understand how valuable time is to our customers and our team members. That’s one reason why Quickease is a great place to work. We’re always looking for new team members to help us achieve our goals.

If you are interested in becoming a Quickease Team Member, please submit an application.

    Requirements:

  • Must be 16 years or older.
  • Must be able to pass a drug test.
  • Must Be detail oriented.

    Preferred candidates will:

  • Have exceptional customer service skills.
  • Work with a sense of urgency.
  • Be willing to work alone or in a team environment.
  • Have excellent communication skills.
  • Always be willing to learn and improve.

We are an equal opportunity employer. Applicants are considered for positions without regard to veteran status, uniformed service member status, race, religion, sex, national origin, age, physical or mental disability.

You Can Trust our Detailers!

Quickease Team Members are IDA certified and provide only the safest and most detailed services available.
